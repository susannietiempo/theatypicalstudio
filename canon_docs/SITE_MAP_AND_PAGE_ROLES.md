# SITE MAP & PAGE ROLES — The Atypical Studio
**Marketing Website (v1) — Selective Partnership**

This document defines the **final sitemap**, **page intent**, **content role**, and **animation density**
for each page. It is a source of truth for AI-driven implementation.

---

## Global Intent

- Optimize for **selective partnership**, not lead volume
- Enable **self-qualification** through clarity and boundaries
- Speak directly to the **hybrid operator** (founder + builder)
- Use motion to support hierarchy, never to decorate

---

## Top-Level Navigation

Primary navigation:
- Home
- Ways of Working
- How We Partner
- Approach Stories
- About
- **Start a conversation** (primary CTA)

---

## Page-by-Page Definition

---

## `/` — Home

### Primary Role
- Position the brand
- Establish authority and tone
- Filter for fit
- Invite conversation

### Key Sections
1. Hero (dark section)
2. What We Do (brief framing, not a service list)
3. Ways of Working (pillar cards)
4. For You / Not For You (explicit filter)
5. Selected Approach Stories (2–3)
6. How We Partner (snapshot)
7. Primary CTA band
8. Footer

### Animation Density
- **Medium**
- Hero: one subtle emphasis animation
- Section reveals and staggered cards allowed
- No motion in dense reading areas

---

## `/ways-of-working`

### Primary Role
- Replace traditional “Services” with mindset + engagement clarity
- Set expectations early

### Key Sections
1. Intro manifesto (dark or high-contrast)
2. Ways of Working (3–4 blocks)
   - When it fits
   - How we show up
   - What it looks like in practice
3. What We Don’t Do (boundaries)
4. CTA: Start a conversation

### Animation Density
- **Low–Medium**
- Section-based reveals only
- No animation inside long-form paragraphs

---

## `/how-we-partner`

### Primary Role
- Reduce uncertainty for serious prospects
- Show what working together actually feels like

### Key Sections
1. What partnership means (tone-setting)
2. Engagement model (phases, not steps)
3. Communication & collaboration norms
4. Mutual expectations
5. CTA

### Animation Density
- **Low**
- Mostly static
- Motion limited to section entrances

---

## `/approach-stories` (Index)

### Primary Role
- Proof through thinking, not performance
- Allow visitors to assess fit through real scenarios

### Key Sections
1. Intro framing (why these stories exist)
2. Story grid/list (ApproachStoryCard)
3. CTA reinforcement

### Framing Rules
- Stories are **public**
- Framed as “written for teams considering partnership”
- No metrics-heavy claims
- No enterprise posturing

### Animation Density
- **Low–Medium**
- Staggered card reveals allowed
- No hero animation

---

## `/approach-stories/[slug]` (Detail)

### Primary Role
- Demonstrate depth of thinking and collaboration style

### Required Structure
1. Context
2. Our Approach
3. Outcome (shape, not metrics)
4. Why It Mattered

### Content Rules
- 200–400 words
- Calm, narrative tone
- Anonymization allowed
- CTA placed after the story, not before

### Animation Density
- **Minimal**
- Reading-first
- No hero animation
- No motion inside body text

---

## `/about`

### Primary Role
- Humanize the brand
- Explain “why Atypical” without ego

### Key Sections
1. Who We Are
2. Why “Atypical” Matters
3. Values in Practice
4. How We Think About Work

### Animation Density
- **Low**
- Editorial pacing
- No expressive motion

---

## `/start-a-conversation`

### Primary Role
- Intentional conversion
- Fit-check, not sales push

### Key Sections
1. Short framing (“If this feels right…”)
2. Contact form
3. What happens next (expectation-setting)

### Content Rules
- Calm, respectful language
- No urgency
- No multi-step funnel language

### Animation Density
- **Minimal**
- Focus on clarity and usability

---

## `/privacy` and `/terms`

### Primary Role
- Legal compliance

### Animation Density
- **None**

---

## Global Animation Rules (Reaffirmed)

- Motion supports hierarchy only
- No parallax or looping effects
- Respect reduced-motion preferences
- Long-form reading zones remain static

---

## Scope Boundaries

- ❌ No blog / insights at launch
- ❌ No gated content
- ❌ No enterprise-style case studies
- ❌ No feature or service catalogs
